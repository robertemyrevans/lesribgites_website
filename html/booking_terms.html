CANCELLATIONS/ALTERATIONS
 
If for any reason you wish to cancel your booking, please advise by telephone as soon as possible and then confirm by email or post.  We will always try to re-let to minimise your loss and, if you advise us before the final balance is due, we retain only your deposit. Should you cancel after the final balance has been paid, we will attempt to re-let and, if successful, we will refund the full amount less the 25% deposit.  If for any reason you wish to change your booking and another date is possible, a small administration fee of 15 euros will be charged. But if this is not possible, the original holiday remains booked and cancellation conditions apply.  You are strongly advised to take out a comprehensive travel insurance policy to include cancellation cover and to have full cover for the party’s personal belongings and public liability.

Although our pools comply with stringent French safety standards, it is the responsibility of our guests to ensure their own safety and that of their children around the pools.  Please note that the pools are not heated and are decommissioned between the end of September and mid May each year.
 
PLEASE NOTE:   In common with most of rural France we have septic tanks and so only natural body waste and French toilet paper may be flushed (no plastic or rubber goods or English toilet paper, which is apparently too harsh!). Also, only septic tank toilet cleaners should be used.  An initial supply of toilet paper and cleaner will be provided

OTHER INFORMATION
 
The gite is available from 4pm on the first day of your holiday. Please advise us if you will be arriving later than 8pm.  The gite is to be vacated by 10am on the day of departure, unless otherwise agreed. Duvet and bedlinen are provided for each bed for the duration of your stay.  If an extra change of bedding is required there is a charge of 6 euros per bed per week, payable at time of change.
