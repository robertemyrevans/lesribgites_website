Sunset

Sunset is a converted barn built from the local limestone. It has its own pool, positioned to get the sun all day long.

In the afternoon and evening the courtyard is bathed in sunshine and you can sit and enjoy the beautiful and often spectacular sunsets under the pergola.

  • We have taken care to retain the original features, including exposed beams and stonework. The downstairs living area is spacious. The kitchen has a gas cooker, microwave, large fridge/freezer, washing machine, dishwasher, crockery and cooking utensils. The dining table and chairs seat 6 comfortably. The lounge area has 2 settees, coffee table, TV, DVD and video.
  • Sunset has three bedrooms (double/twin/bunkbeds) and a bathroom with shower and toilet. The beds will be made up on your arrival with all cotton bedding.
  • The private walled courtyard has a barbecue and the garden table and chairs are under a pergola so you can enjoy alfresco meals or enjoy a drink while the sun sets.
  • Sunset has its own parking area.
  • We ask that guests do not smoke in our gites.
