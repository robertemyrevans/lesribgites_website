How to book

1. If you would like to book with us, please contact us to verify availability.

2. We can take provisional bookings by telephone or email. Provisional bookings are held for 7 days. However, to secure the booking we require you to fill in and return our booking form below, together with a 25% deposit and a cheque for the £100 breakages deposit.

3. Payment of the deposit can be made directly into our UK bank account, details of which will be supplied.

4. Confirmation will be sent to you by email or post, when the deposit is received.

5. The final balance becomes due 8 weeks before the start of your holiday. We will remind you by e-mail or letter shortly before the due date. This payment can also be made directly into our bank account.

6. We reserve the right to re-let the property should you fail to pay the final balance on time. In this case you would lose your deposit.

7. On receipt of the final balance we will send you a map with directions to Les Ribaudieres.

8. Please ensure that you read the booking terms and conditions below.
