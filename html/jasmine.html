Jasmine

Jasmine is our most recently converted barn. It has its own 11m private pool. Jasmine's courtyard faces almost due south, so it enjoys sunshine for most of the day.

However, its 2 foot thick walls and vine covered pergola ensure a cool retreat from the heat of the day.

  • Jasmine has a comfortable sitting area with 2 sofas, TV and DVD player. There is wifi on site. However, the signal at present is not strong - the 2ft. thick walls mean that the connection is patchy and cannot be guaranteed.
  • The interior has exposed beams and stonework and the original fireplace, with a log burner.
  • The kitchen area is well equipped with a gas cooker and grill, a large fridge/freezer, dishwasher and microwave, all crockery and cooking utensils.
  • Jasmine sleeps 7 and has three bedrooms, a double, a twin and a triple. The beds will be made up on your arrival with all cotton bedding. The bathroom is downstairs and has a shower and toilet.
  • Jasmine has its own parking area.
  • We ask that guests do not smoke in our gites.
