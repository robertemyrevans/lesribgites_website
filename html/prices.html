Arrival evening meal.

On your arrival night, we are delighted to be able to offer you a 3 course meal including wine, cooked and delivered to your gite(s). When you arrive tired and hungry after your journey, you won't have to think about shopping and cooking for your first home cooked French meal.

The cost for this is 17 euro per adult and 12 euro per child under 12. Specific menus will be decided nearer dates of arrival, because we intend to use seasonal ingredients whenever possible.

If you wish to take advantage of this option, please let us know in the 'Any special requirements' section of the booking form.
