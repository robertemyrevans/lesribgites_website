Our Pools

There are two pools here at Les Ribaudieres. Each gite has its own private pool.

Jasmine has its own swimming pool (11m by 4.5m) with sunbathing terrace. Sunset has its own pool (8m by 4.5m) and sunbathing terrace.

The pools are of a similar design with steps at the shallow end which are ideal for toddlers to splash about in while supervised. The deep end is 2m.

POOL SAFETY

Our pools have security fencing which conforms with stringent French safety standards. However, it is the responsibility of our guests to ensure their own safety and that of their children around the pools.

Please note that the pools are not heated and are decommissioned from the end of September to mid May each year.
